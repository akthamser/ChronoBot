# ChronoBot: Journey Through History
Welcome to the GitHub repository for ChronoBot: Journey Through History! This repository contains all the resources and code for our immersive and captivating video game.


## Description: 

In “ChronoBot: Journey Through History”, players take on the role of ChronoBot, a time-traveling robot created by the scientist Dr. Tari. The primary objective is to prevent catastrophic historical disasters and alter the course of history by time-traveling minutes before the disaster occurs. In the first chapter, the mission focuses on stopping the Chernobyl nuclear disaster. ChronoBot must navigate the nuclear station with stealth and intellect, solving intriguing puzzles in each room, ultimately reaching the reactor to prevent its explosion in the span of 6 minutes. The game combines elements of action-adventure, puzzle-solving, historical themes, stealth, and time travel, resulting in a captivating gaming experience.



### Main Features:

* Time Travel Mechanics: Travel through time and explore different historical periods, 
     witnessing important events and their impacts on history.
* Puzzle-Solving Gameplay: Encounter challenging puzzles in each level of the game, 
     requiring logical thinking and keen observation  to advance.
* Stealth and Strategy: Employ stealth tactics to sneak past guards and security systems, 
     maintaining a low profile as you navigate through different spots.
* Engaging Storyline: Immerse yourself in an exciting narrative as you uncover the 
     mysteries surrounding these historical disasters.
* Historical Accuracy: Experience researched environments and events, providing an authentic representation of each timeline and its surroundings.


### Implementation technologies:


* Game Engine: Unity.

* Integrated Development Environment (IDE): Visual Studio.

* Version Control: Plastic SCM and GitHub.

* Design Softwares: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects.

* Game UI: Figma.

* 3D Modeling Software: Blender.
